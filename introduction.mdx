## Overview

Welcome to Doctopus! Doctopus is the world's first documentation browser purpose built to help developers navigate documentation more efficiently, find answers to questions faster, and improve overall developer experience.

If you have any questions at all please join our **[discord](https://https://discord.gg/RdUpqq9t6V)** or feel free to shoot us an email any time at **[hey@doctopus.ai](mailto:hey@doctopus.ai)**!

## Account Signup

Welcome to Doctopus, the one-stop platform for managing NodeJS package documentation with the power of AI. Here's how to get started:

1. Launch Doctopus and head to the signup window.

2. Create an account using Clerk's secure account management system.

3. You can sign up with your email, Google account, or GitHub credentials. Just choose your preferred method and follow the prompts.
