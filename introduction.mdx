## Account Signup

Welcome to Doctopus, the one-stop platform for managing NodeJS package documentation with the power of AI. Here's how to get started:

- Launch Doctopus and head to the signup window.

- Create an account using Clerk's secure account management system.

- You can sign up with your email, Google account, or GitHub credentials. Just choose your preferred method and follow the prompts.
