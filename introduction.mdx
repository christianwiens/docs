## Account Signup

Welcome to Doctopus, the one-stop platform for managing NodeJS package documentation with the power of AI. Here's how to get started:

- Launch Doctopus and head to the signup window.

- Create an account using Clerk's secure account management system.

- You can sign up with your email, Google account, or GitHub credentials. Just choose your preferred method and follow the prompts.

<img
  src="/images/accountsignup.png"
  alt="Header Docs"
/>

## Creating a New Workspace

- Once signed up, access the app where you'll be prompted to create a new workspace.

- Upload your project to Doctopus. This allows the app to identify your Node dependencies and gather the corresponding documentation.

<img
  src="/images/accountsignup.png"
  alt="Header Docs"
/>

## Selecting Your Packages

- After uploading your project, a new window appears showing all detected packages.

- Select or deselect packages to customize your Doctopus workspace.

- Click "Get Started" to confirm your package selection and proceed.

- At any time you can select or deselect packages from this menu to fit your current needs. Click "save changes" and the documentation for any packages you select will automatically appear on the left sidebar of the app now.

<img
  src="/images/packageselect.png"
  alt="Header Docs"
/>

## Navigating Your Doctopus Workspace

1. Your workspace is your hub within Doctopus, featuring a sidebar with all selected packages for quick access.

2. Navigate between packages by clicking their icons on the sidebar.

3. Open sub-pages within specific package docs in separate tabs. Easily switch between packages, and your tabs will be preserved.

4. In the top right corner you'll find a button to activate the Octobot, as well as a Settings menu.

<img
  src="/images/mainscreen.png"
  alt="Header Docs"
/>

## Utilizing Octobot AI (In Development)

1. In the right sidebar of the app you will find Octobot, the Doctopus AI that uniquely understands your project and the documentation for any packages you’re using.

2. You can open and close the Octobot by selecting the 🐙 emoji in the top right of the window. When Octobot is active a ✨ will appear over the octopus.

3. You can use Octobot AI to ask questions, generate code examples, and much more.

> Note: Octobot is in early development and some features are unavailable in the current release.
